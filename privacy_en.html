2. Personal data collected

The club may collect the following types of data:

  • Contact data (name, surname, email, phone).

3. Purposes of processing

Data is processed for the following purposes:

a) Sports and administrative management of the club

  • Registration in competitions, processing of licenses and insurance.
  • Communications with members, players, and families.
  • Organization of training sessions, matches, and club activities.

b) Informational communications

  • Sending notices related to the operation of the club.
  • Dissemination of calendars, schedules, calls, and urgent notices.

c) Audiovisual content

  • Publication of images or videos on the web, social networks, or club material, always respecting the right to one's own image.
  • In the case of minors, prior consent from legal guardians will be requested when necessary.

d) Handling requests through forms or email

  • Response to queries sent by users.

4. Legitimation of processing

The legal bases allowing processing are:

  • Execution of the sports and associative relationship between the club and its members.
  • Compliance with legal obligations (processing before sports federations, mandatory insurance, etc.).
  • Consent of the interested party, especially in communication of non-essential data and images.
  • Legitimate interest, such as organizational security and internal communication.
